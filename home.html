My Story

My healing journey began when I was an infant and had to undergo open heart surgery. I have had massive physical heart healing, having now journeyed through 3 open hearts and other multiple microscopic surgeries, along with an over 200lb weight loss journey. I have been on the self-work path since I was sent to a somewhat therapeutic boarding school at age 14, right after my Grandfather died and opened the portal for my gift of sight to flood in. I believe my physical healing journey along with my psychic abilities naturally led me to this work.

That moment came in the fall of 2015 when I was on an intimate women’s business retreat with my then coach and a woman who I would come to know as my healing mentor and close friend Celeste Lemieux. I was rooming together in a loft-style space with one of my closest friends and towards the end of retreat one night I was up in my loft area getting ready for bed when these two rushed into the room. In hearing the commotion I looked down from my space to see my friend receiving an exorcism. As I held space for this massive clearing and healing taking place I knew this was the work I would eventually be offering. I shifted into motherhood and continued my spiritual and healing journey behind the scenes, continuing to learn from my mentors.
